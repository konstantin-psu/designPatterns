CS510DP Design Patterns, Winter 2000
Postmortem
Last updated Apr 17, 2000


From time to time, students seek my advice to select which courses to take. My initial advice is always to get all the available information about each course that they are considering. In this spirit, I provide the following information about CS510DP, Design Patterns (in Java), offered in the Winter 2000 term. This material, in addition to the current syllabus available from my homepage, is intended to enable prospective students to make an informed decision before enrolling into new offerings of this course.

Syllabus The syllabus was made available several months before the beginning of the course. The syllabus links handouts, assignments with complete solutions, and relevant references. The document linked here is the most recent version for the Winter 2000 term.
Exam The linked document is the complete text of the exam. This version of the exam is without answers to give students an opportunity to work on the exam and compare their answers with those provided by the instructor.
Key The linked document is a copy of the preceding one augmented with answers. The answers have no pretension of completeness or even absolute correctness. Rather, they show what suffices to comfortably pass the exam.
Grades The linked document shows the grade distribution for the entire class.
Feedback The linked document, and subdocuments, contain the answers to a questionnaire filled by the students at the end of the course. The questionnaire was prepared by the instructor. A similar, less detailed, questionnaire is required by the department, but it is not made public. Filling the questionnaire was voluntary. Not all the students answered all the questions. The answers were provided by the students anonymously. The answers in the linked document have been copied verbatim by the instructor from the student forms. In a few places, averages have been added.
Evaluations Standard evaluation collected by the department.
